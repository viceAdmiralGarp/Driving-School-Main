ABOUT US

We are glad to welcome you to the website of the driving school in Dnipro “Grand”.

With us you will learn how to efficiently drive a car with a manual or automatic transmission.

Our driving instructors will help you pass the traffic police exam and obtain licenses of categories A, B, C, D, E. Driving courses are held in the city center and on the left bank within walking distance from any transport.

You can also choose a convenient time to attend theoretical classes at our driving school.
